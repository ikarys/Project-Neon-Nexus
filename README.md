# Project-Neon-Nexus
Project Neon Nexus is a cyberpunk-themed rogue-like game that combines isometric action with cumulative progression. 
Take on the role of Nyx, a skilled hacker, as you navigate through procedurally generated levels, battle futuristic enemies, and uncover a deep cybernetic mystery. 
Explore neon-lit streets, hack into terminals, and wield an arsenal of cybernetic weaponry. 
Progress through unique characters, unlock new abilities, and challenge formidable bosses. 
Immerse yourself in a pixel art world with dynamic lighting and electronic soundscapes. 
Prepare for an intense journey through the futuristic underworld of Project Neon Nexus.
